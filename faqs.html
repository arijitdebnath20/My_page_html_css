"A device that will change the scenario of visually impared persons"

  • Produce Type : Digital Braille Keyboard.
  • Product Name : Smart Typing System For Visually Impared Persons.
  • Product Invented : 3rd March 2020.
  • Product Cost : 799 rupees (roughly).
  • Team Designation : Student of JIS college of Engineering, kalyani, West Bengal.
  • Components Used : 6 piezo sensors, 1 uno arduino board, connecting wires, 1 wooden board for mounting the components.
  • What is Braille Keyboard : It is specially designed keyboard for blind and visually impared persons by using Braille dots.
